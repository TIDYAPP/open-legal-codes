SEC. 2.181. - Reporting of independent expenditures.

a.

Any person making independent expenditure(s) in a local election of a cumulative amount of five hundred dollars ($500.00) or more within the ninety (90) day period preceding a local election shall report all independent expenditures to the city clerk on Fair Political Practices Commission (FPPC) Form 496, or a similar form as designated by the FPPC to report independent expenditures during an election, and which shall be filed with the city clerk in a manner as described in the FPP 496 form instructions.

b.

An independent expenditure subject to reporting as set forth in subsection a. shall be filed with the city clerk no later than twenty-four (24) hours after the expenditure is made.

(Ord. No. 5.22, § 7, 4/26/22.)
