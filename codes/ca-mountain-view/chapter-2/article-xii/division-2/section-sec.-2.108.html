SEC. 2.108. - Updates to and requirement to update the top contributors list.

If the order of top contributors required to be disclosed pursuant to this division changes or a new contributor qualifies as a top contributor, the disclosure in the advertisement shall be updated to reflect the new top contributors within five (5) business days unless the advertisement cannot be updated except by a subsequent order for production or printing, in which case the disclosure shall be updated prior to placing a new or modified order for additional printing of the advertisement.

(Ord. No. 8.16, § 1, 6/14/16.)
