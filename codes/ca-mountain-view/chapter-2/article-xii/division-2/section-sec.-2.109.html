SEC. 2.109. - Criminal enforcement.

Any person who knowingly, willfully or negligently violates any provisions of this division is guilty of a misdemeanor. Any person who causes any other person to violate any provision of this division, or who aids and abets any other person in the violation of any provision of this division, shall be liable under the provisions of this division. Any monetary penalty for such a violation shall be the maximum amount permitted by law.

(Ord. No. 8.16, § 1, 6/14/16; Ord. No. 5.22, § 4, 4/26/22.)
