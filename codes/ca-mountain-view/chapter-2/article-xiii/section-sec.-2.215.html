SEC. 2.215. - Postaward.

a.

Bid protests. The purchasing agent may alter and/or shorten the normal bid protest process if emergency circumstances will not permit a delay resulting from the normal bid protest process, but such period shall be no less than twenty-four (24) hours.

b.

Authority to cancel procurements. The city manager or designee has the authority to rescind a purchase contract for nonperformance within twenty-four (24) hours when a contractor or vendor, once awarded a contract, is unable to immediately perform under the terms of the contract.

(Ord. No. 7.16, § 1, 5/24/16; Ord. No. 5.2025, § 6, 6/24/25.)
