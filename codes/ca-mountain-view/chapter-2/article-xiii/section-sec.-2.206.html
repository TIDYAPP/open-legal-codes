SEC. 2.206. - Encumbrance of funds.

Except in cases of emergency, when necessary for the protection of public peace, health, safety, welfare or property or interest of the city or general public, the purchasing agent shall not issue any purchase order for supplies, materials, equipment or certain services unless there exists an unencumbered appropriation in the account group against which such purchase is to be charged.

(Ord. No. 7.16, § 1, 5/24/16.)
