SEC. 2.9. - Authorization to disburse salaries—Regular employees.

The director of finance is authorized and directed to disburse every alternate week, warrants for the payment of all accrued salaries of such officers and employees of the city as are regularly elected, appointed or employed.

(Code 1938, Sec. 100; Ord. No. 175.197, 1/14/53; Ord. No. 25.65, 6/28/65.)
