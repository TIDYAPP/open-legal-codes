SEC. 2.8. - Same—Relief of calamity or disaster.*

Nothing stated in Sec. 2.4 to 2.7 shall be construed to prohibit the use of city-owned equipment, tools or paraphernalia for the relief of public or private calamity or disaster when authorized by a city official.

(Ord. No. 175.266, 7/14/54.)

* As to civil defense and disaster generally, see Chapter 11 of this Code.
