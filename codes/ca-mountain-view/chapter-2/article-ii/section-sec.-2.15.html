SEC. 2.15. - Subpoena power.

In any investigation or hearing conducted in accordance with the personnel rules and regulations, any lawfully authorized hearing body conducting such investigation or hearing shall have the power to examine witnesses under oath and compel their attendance or the production of evidence before it by subpoena issued in the name of the city. Refusal of a person to produce documents, attend or testify in answer to such subpoena shall subject such person to prosecution in the manner set forth by law for failure to appear before the city council in response to a subpoena issued by the city council.

(Ord. No. 175.272, 8/11/54; Ord. No. 9.86, 3/18/86.)
