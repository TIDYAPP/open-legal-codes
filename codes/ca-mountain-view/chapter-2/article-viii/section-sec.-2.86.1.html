SEC. 2.86.1. - General municipal elections—Time for holding.

Pursuant to Charter Section 1300, general municipal elections for the election of city officers and such other purposes as the council may prescribe shall be held in the city on the first Tuesday after the first Monday in November of each even-numbered year, commencing with the year 1994. Such general municipal elections shall be consolidated with statewide elections held on the same date.

(Ord. No. 1.94, 1/11/94.)
