SEC. 2.65. - Time for payment of gross receipts percentages, penalty for nonpayment.*

If the franchise is a renewal of a right already in existence, payment of the percentage of gross receipts shall begin at once upon the granting of the franchise. If the franchise is not a renewal of a right already in existence, and is for a term in excess of five (5) years, the city council may in its discretion provide that no percentage shall be paid for the first five (5) years succeeding the date of the franchise, but thereafter the percentage shall be payable annually. If payment is not made, the franchise shall be forfeited.

(Ord. No. 175.189, 11/12/52.)

* For general provision as to payment of percentage of gross receipts, see Char., Sec. 1206.
