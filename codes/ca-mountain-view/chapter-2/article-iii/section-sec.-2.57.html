SEC. 2.57. - Inserting discriminatory clause in franchise or grant prohibited.

No clause or condition of any kind shall be inserted in any franchise or grant offered or sold under the terms of this ordinance or city Charter which shall directly or indirectly restrict free and open competition in bidding therefore, should the city council in its discretion elect to advertise for bids; and no clause or provision shall be inserted in any franchise offered for sale, which shall in anywise favor one person as against another, in bidding for the purchase thereof, should the city council in its discretion elect to so advertise for bids.

(Ord. No. 175.189, 11/12/52.)
