SEC. 2.69.9. - Same—Procedures after arrest.

In making any arrest as authorized by this division, a community service officer shall follow those procedures set forth in Sec. 1.8 of Chapter 1 of this Code, the provisions of which are incorporated herein by reference as if fully set forth at this point. Whenever, in the said Sec. 1.8 of Chapter 1 of this Code, the terms "arresting officer" or "officer" are used, such terms shall mean and include any person occupying the position of "community service officer."

(Ord. No. 15.79, 4/9/79.)
