SEC. 2.69.10. - Effect on arrests by peace officers or private persons.

The provisions of this division shall have no effect on the procedures required to be utilized by a peace officer, as the term "police officer" is defined in Sections 831-830.35 of the Penal Code of the state, in making arrests for the commission of any public offense, nor shall the provisions of this division apply to arrests made by private persons for the commission of any public offense.

(Ord. No. 15.79, 4/9/79.)
