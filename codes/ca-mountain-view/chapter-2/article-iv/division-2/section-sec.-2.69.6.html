SEC. 2.69.6. - Creation of position of community service officer; duties.

Within the police department of the city is the position of "community service officer." It shall be the duty of any person occupying this position to:

(a)

Enforce all provisions of the Mountain View City Code and any ordinance enacted by the city council of the city which is not codified into this Code.

(b)

Enforce all provisions of state law declared to be misdemeanors or infractions, including all state vehicle laws applicable to street traffic in the city which may be assigned to such officer by the chief of police pursuant to subsection a. of Sec. 19.28 of Article 11 of Chapter 19 of this Code; and

(c)

Perform such other duties or functions which may be assigned or delegated to such officer by the chief of police pursuant to Sec. 1.14 of Chapter 1 of this Code.

(Ord. No. 15.79, 4/9/79.)
