SEC. 2.69.7. - Same—Deemed to be a public officer; immunity.

Any person occupying the position of community service officer shall be deemed to be a public officer, as the term "public officer" is defined by Sec. 836.5 of the Penal Code of the state. There shall be no civil liability on the part of, and no cause of action shall arise against, any such community service officer acting pursuant to the provisions of this article and within the scope of his or her authority, for false arrest or false imprisonment arising out of any arrest which is lawful or which the community service officer, at the time of arrest, had reasonable cause to believe was lawful. No such officer shall be deemed an aggressor or lose his or her right to self-defense by the use of reasonable force to effect the arrest, prevent escape or overcome resistance.

(Ord. No. 15.79, 4/9/79.)
