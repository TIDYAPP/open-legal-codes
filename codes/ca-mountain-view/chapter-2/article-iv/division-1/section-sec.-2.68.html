SEC. 2.68. - Same—Authority and powers.

In all matters pertaining to the enforcement of the police regulations of the city each police officer of the city shall have and exercise all the authority and powers that are now, or may be hereafter, conferred by law on peace officers of the state. They shall also have such further powers as are now, or may hereafter be, prescribed by ordinance, or by order of the council.

(Code 1938, Sec. 193; Ord. No. 15.79, 4/9/79.)
