SEC. 2.67. - Members—Rights, privileges, immunities, etc.

In all matters pertaining to the enforcement of the police regulations of the city each police officer of the city shall have and enjoy all the rights, privileges, immunities and protection that are now, or may be hereafter, conferred by law on peace officers of the state.

(Code 1938, Sec. 193; Ord. No. 15.79, 4/9/79.)
