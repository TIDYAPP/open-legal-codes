SEC. 2.69. - Same—Duties.*

Each police officer of the city shall enforce the police regulations of the city, prevent the commission of any breach of the peace, suppress riots and disorderly assemblages, and arrest every person found violating any law or ordinance, or committing any act injurious to the quiet and good order of the city, or to the person or property of any resident thereof.

(Code 1938, Sec. 194; Ord. No. 15.79, 4/9/79.)

* For additional provisions as to powers and duties of chief of police, See Section 715, Article VII of the Charter.
