SEC. 2.69.2. - Acceptance of state aid for selection and training of public safety communicators.

a.

The City of Mountain View declares that it desires to qualify to receive aid from the State of California under the provisions of Sections 13522 and 13525, Chapter 1, of Title 4, Part 4, of the California Penal Code.

b.

Pursuant to Section 13510(c) of the California Penal Code, the City of Mountain View will adhere to standards for recruitment and training of public safety communicators established by the California Commission on Peace Officer Standards and Training (POST).

c.

Pursuant to Section 13512 of the California Penal Code, the commission and its representatives may make such inquiries as deemed appropriate by the commission to ascertain that the City of Mountain View's public communicator personnel adhere to standards for selection and training established by the Commission on Peace Officer Standards and Training (POST).

d.

For the purposes of this section, public safety communicator shall be defined to include nonpeace officer personnel employed full-time or part-time by the city to perform duties which include as a primary responsibility receiving emergency calls for law enforcement services and/or dispatching law enforcement personnel.

(Ord. 22.90, 11/27/90.)
