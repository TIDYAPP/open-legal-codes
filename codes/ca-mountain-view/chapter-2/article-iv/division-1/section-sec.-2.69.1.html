SEC. 2.69.1. - Acceptance of state aid for training peace officers.

The city council, on behalf of the city, declares that it desires to qualify to receive aid from the state under the provisions of Chapter 1 of Title 4, Part 4 of the California Penal Code. Pursuant to Section 13522 of the California Penal Code, the city while receiving aid from the state pursuant to the state law herein referred to, will adhere to the standards for recruitment and training established by the commission on peace officers standards and training of the state.

(Ord. No. 175.639, 12/12/60; Ord. No. 15.79, 4/9/79.)
