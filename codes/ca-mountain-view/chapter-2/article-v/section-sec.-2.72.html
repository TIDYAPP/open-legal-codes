SEC. 2.72. - Expenditure of moneys in fund.

All moneys in the fund created by Sec. 2.70 shall be expended exclusively for the purposes authorized by, and subject to the provisions of Article 5, Chapter 1 , Division 1 of the Streets and Highways Code of the State, as amended.

(Code 1938, Sec. 449.)

* See Sts. & H. C. A., Sec. 181 et seq. As to streets and sidewalks generally, see Chap. 27 of this Code. As to taxation generally, see Chap. 29.
