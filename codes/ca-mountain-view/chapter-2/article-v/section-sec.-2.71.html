SEC. 2.71. - Moneys to be paid into fund.

All moneys received from the state under the provisions of the California Streets and Highways Code of the state for the use of this city in the acquisition of real property or interests therein, the construction, improvement or maintenance of streets and highways other than state highways, and transit facilities shall be paid into such fund.

(Code 1938, Sec. 448; Ord. No. 5.99, 4/13/99.)
