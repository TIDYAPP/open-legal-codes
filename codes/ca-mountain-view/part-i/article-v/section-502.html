Section 502. - Councilmember to hold no other office.

No member of the council shall hold any other city office or city employment except as is otherwise provided by this Charter. No member of the council shall be eligible to be elected or appointed to any city position, office or employment which was created or the compensation of which was increased by the council while that person was a member thereof, until one year after the expiration of the term for which the councilmember was elected or appointed.

(As amended June 3, 1980.)
