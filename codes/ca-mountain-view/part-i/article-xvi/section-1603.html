Section 1603. - Construction of Charter.

Unless the provisions or the context otherwise require, as used in this Charter:

(a)

"Shall" is mandatory, and "may" is permissive.

(b)

"City" is the City of Mountain View and "department", "board", "commission", "agency", "officer", or "employee", is a department, board, commission, agency, officer or employee, as the case may be, of the City of Mountain View.

(c)

"Council" is the city council of the City of Mountain View.

(d)

"County" is the County of Santa Clara.

(e)

"State" is the State of California.

(As amended April 9, 1974; June 3, 1980.)
