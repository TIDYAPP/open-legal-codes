Section 905. - Compensation; vacancies.

The members of the boards and commissions shall serve without compensation for their services as such, but may receive reimbursement for necessary traveling and other expenses incurred on official duty when such expenditures have received authorization by the council. Any vacancies on any board or commission, from whatever cause arising, shall be filled by appointment by the council. Upon a vacancy occurring, leaving an unexpired portion of a term, any appointment to fill such vacancy shall be for the unexpired portion of such term. If a member of a board or commission is absent from three regular meetings of such board or commission, consecutively, or within a calendar quarter, or is convicted of a crime involving moral turpitude, or ceases to be a qualified elector and resident of the city, that office shall become vacant upon the declaration of council. The council may, for good cause, determine that a vacancy has not been created.

(As amended by election June 3, 1980; November 3, 1998.)
