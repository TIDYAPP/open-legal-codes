Section 1003. - Political activities prohibited; discrimination.

No employee of the city shall, while in uniform or during that employee's city working hours, take an active part in any municipal or other political campaign, nor seek or accept contributions for or against any candidate or issue therein, nor seek or accept signatures to any petition for or against any such candidate or issue. Nothing in this section shall be construed to prevent any such persons from seeking election or appointment to public office.

Except as otherwise provided by the general laws of this state heretofore or hereafter enacted, no person in the classified service, or seeking admission thereto, shall be employed, promoted, demoted or discharged or in any way favored or discriminated against because of political opinions or affiliations or because of race or religious belief.

(As amended April 9, 1974; June 3, 1980.)
