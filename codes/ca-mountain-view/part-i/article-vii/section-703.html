Section 703. - Compensation.

The compensation of all city officers and employees, except as otherwise provided in this Charter, shall be by salary to be fixed for ordinance or resolution. No officer or employee shall be allowed any fees, perquisites, emoluments, rewards or compensation, aside from the salary or compensation as fixed by the council, but all fees received by that person in connection with that person's official duties shall be paid by that person into the city treasury.

(As amended June 3, 1980.)
