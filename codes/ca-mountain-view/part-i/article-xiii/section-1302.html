Section 1302. - Procedure for holding elections.

Except as hereinafter provided, all elections shall be held in accordance with the provisions of the Elections Code of the State of California, as the same now exists or may hereafter be amended, for the holding of elections in general law cities, insofar as the same are not in conflict with this Charter. In all municipal elections, the city council may appoint a canvassing board, which shall meet on the morning of the first Tuesday following the election and canvass the returns and certify the results thereof to the city council.

(As amended November 6, 1962.)
