SEC. 3.8. - Printing name and address of distributor or sponsor on handbills.

No person shall distribute, deposit, scatter, hand out or circulate any commercial or noncommercial handbill in any place, under any circumstances, which does not have printed on the cover, front or back thereof, the name and address of the person who caused the same to be distributed; provided, however, that in the case of a fictitious person or club, in addition to such fictitious name, the true names and addresses of the owners, managers or agents of the person sponsoring such handbill shall also appear thereon.

(Ord. No. 175.481, 6/30/58.)
