SEC. 3.6. - Distributing, etc., on premises without owner's consent prohibited.*

No person shall distribute, deposit, place, throw, scatter or cast any commercial or noncommercial handbill upon any private premises when the owner or occupant of said premises has expressed an objection to such distribution, depositing, placement, throwing, scattering or casting; nor shall any person so distribute, deposit, place, throw, scatter or cast any such handbill without securing or otherwise preventing such handbill from being blown or drifted about such premises or elsewhere.

(Ord. No. 175.481, 6/30/58; Ord. No. 1.72, 1/10/72.)

* As to leaving drugs, etc., on the door steps, etc., see Sec. 21.15 of this code.
