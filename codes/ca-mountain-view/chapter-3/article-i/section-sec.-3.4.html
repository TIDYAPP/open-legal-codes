SEC. 3.4. - Placing in vehicles prohibited; exception.

No person shall distribute, deposit, place, throw, scatter or cast any commercial or noncommercial handbill in or upon any automobile or other vehicle. The provisions of this section shall not be deemed to prohibit the handing, transmitting or distributing of any noncommercial handbill to the owner or other occupant of any automobile or other vehicle, who is willing to accept the same.

(Ord. No. 175.481, 6/30/58.)
