SEC. 3.3. - Public distribution, etc., prohibited; exception.

No person shall distribute, deposit, place, throw, scatter or cast any commercial or noncommercial handbill in or upon any public place within the city, or hand out or distribute or sell any commercial handbill in any public place; provided, however, that it shall not be unlawful for any person to hand out or distribute, without charge to the receiver thereof, any noncommercial handbill in any public place to any person willing to accept such noncommercial handbill.

(Ord. No. 175.481, 6/30/58.)
