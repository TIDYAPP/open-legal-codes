SEC. 3.11. - Same—Fees.

License fees under the terms of this chapter, and for any such purpose, shall be as follows:

 For a period of one (1) year, the sum of one hundred (100) dollars.

 For a period of six (6) months, the sum of fifty (50) dollars.

 For a period of one (1) day, the sum of ten (10) dollars.

Provided, that persons acting for licenses, as agents or employees, in the distributing of any such handbills shall not be required to obtain a license or pay a fee, but each such person shall comply with each and all of the other provisions of this chapter and be subject thereto.

(Ord. No. 175.481, 6/30/58.)
