SEC. 3.18.3. - Same—Traffic hazards to freeway users.

No sign shall be painted, attached, installed, erected, constructed or relocated or maintained in the city, if such sign, because of its location, size, nature or type, constitutes or tends to constitute a hazard to the safe and efficient operation of vehicles upon the freeway, or upon any ingress or egress ramps to or from a freeway, or creates a condition which endangers the safety of persons or property thereon.

(Ord. No. 175.622, 8/8/60.)
