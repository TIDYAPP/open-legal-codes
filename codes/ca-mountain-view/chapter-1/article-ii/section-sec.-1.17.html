SEC. 1.17. - Definitions.

For the purposes of this article, the following words shall have the meanings set forth below:

a.

"Enforcement officer" shall mean any city employee or agent of the city designated with the authority to enforce any provision of this code.

b.

"Enforcement official" means the principal supervisor for any city department or division charged with responsibility for enforcement of any provision of this code.

(Ord. No. 12.02, 7/23/02.)
