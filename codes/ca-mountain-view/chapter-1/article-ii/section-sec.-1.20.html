SEC. 1.20. - Hearing officer.

The city manager shall designate a hearing officer for administrative hearings who shall not be the citing enforcement officer. The employment, performance evaluation, compensation and/or benefits of the hearing officer shall not be directly or indirectly conditioned upon the amount of administrative penalties or the rulings upheld, revised or otherwise issued by the hearing officer.

(Ord. No. 12.02, 7/23/02.)
