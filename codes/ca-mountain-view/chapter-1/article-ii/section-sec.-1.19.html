SEC. 1.19. - Amount of administrative penalties.

The amounts of administrative penalties for code violations shall be not less than fifty dollars ($50) nor more than the maximum amount of the penalty(ies) for criminal violations as set forth in the city code. The schedule of administrative penalties shall be published by the city attorney and shall specify the amount of any late payment charges and any increased penalties for repeat violations.

(Ord. No. 12.02, 7/23/02.)
