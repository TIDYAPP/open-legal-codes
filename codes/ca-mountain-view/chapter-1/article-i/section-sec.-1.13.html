SEC. 1.13. - Same—Service of notices generally.

Any notice required to be served or given under any provision of this code shall be deemed to have been served or given when same has been deposited in the United States Post Office at Mountain View, enclosed in a sealed envelope, postage prepaid, addressed to the person sought to be served at his place of business, as same appears in the records of the clerk's office, or if no such address there appears, then to his last-known residence address, and if there be no known residence address, then addressed to such person at Mountain View, California.

(Code 1938, Sec. 9.)
