SEC. 1.15. - Official map.

The survey made by Curtiss M. Barker, licensed surveyor and civil engineer, in December and January, 1902 and 1903, and the map thereof showing the streets, sidewalks and grades, is hereby accepted and adopted as the official survey and map of that part of the city as designated and delineated on such map.

(Code 1938, Sec. 33.)
