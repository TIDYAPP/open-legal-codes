SEC. 5.2. - Animals running at large.

No person owning or having control of any animal shall permit the animal to stray or run at large upon any public street or other public place, or upon any private place or property or common area of any planned development, cluster, townhouse or condominium project, without the consent of the property owner or person in control of the property.

(Ord. No. 4.14, § 1, 4/8/14.)
